BDD specs

1. it does nothing to non-alphabetical characters (3 / 3)
2. it adds "ay" to single letter words beginning with vowel (i / iay)
3. it adds "way" to end of words beginning with vowel (apple / appleway)
  - words beginning with "y" should be treated as consonant (yes / esyay)
4. it takes first letter of words that begin with consonant and moves to back (peter / eterp)
5. it adds "ay" to words that begin with consonants (chrome / omechray)
